# Website Backlog

## Phase 1: Must Have

- Mobile responsiveness
- Improved statistics section
- Resident stories section
- Contact form
- Volunteer registration form
- Donation CTA and flow definition
- Hindi and English language support

## Phase 2: Should Have

- Short videos for residents
- Journey section redesign
- Login system
- Admin panel for content management
- Backend integration for submissions and donations

## Phase 3: Nice To Have

- Open mic activity showcase
- Children interaction activity showcase
- Community engagement gallery
- Newsletter signup
- Basic dashboard for impact stats

## Suggested Acceptance Criteria

- The site works well on mobile and desktop
- Users can understand how to donate or volunteer within one scroll
- The team can update content without editing raw HTML directly
- Resident stories can be added with text, images, and video
- Language selection does not break the layout
